About me

I am an enthusiast of the design of everytday things. I come from a marketing & communicatiosn background, I am now working in coding and I ultimately want to become a UX designer.

Born and raised in Bilbao (Basque Country, Spain), in 2014 I moved to the UK to pursue an undergraduate degree in Busniess and Management at the University of Exeter. Marketing and design related modules where very relevant during my studies.

In 2018, I started my career in the communications department of EUROCONTROL in Brussels. My traineeship was focused on stakeholder relations and information management. After 11 months I decided to jump into a new discipline: coding . I did Le Wagon coding school where I learned using Ruby on Rails, CSS, HTML, JavaScript and key UX practices.

I now work as a full-stack developer at Yuman.io and I am gaining the necessary knowledge and autonomy to build the entire design structure. My goal now is to keep on learning from different fields to build a very multi-disciplinary UX designer profile.
