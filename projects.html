Relevant projects in my studies that concern the discipline of UX

01. Monetizeur

Web application development & design

Monetizeur is a subscription management app that responds to the increasing use of the subscription based services. It aims to help users to get a transparent overview on their expenses and prevent them from making unwanted payments at the same time.

Monetizeur is a web programmed in Ruby on Rails. In this group project my main task was creating the front end. I captured my colleagues' ideas and made the prototypes and wireframes with Figma. I then coded the front-end using HTML, CSS and JavaScript. I was given complete freedom to choose the logo, the colour scheme and style.

02. University of Exeter Design Proposal

Design Strategy Report for my University

This report compiles solutions to three design related issues that concern the University of Exeter. A new logo was proposed to substitude the outdated one in use. The refurbishment of one of the biggest yet oldest buildings in the campus was suggested to provide more working spaces. Finally, the aligment of the University with the Student Guild design was recommended to achieve coherence and brand synergy.

I was in full charge of drafting the new logo proposal by using Photoshop and based the design on feedback from different stakeholder groups. I also drafted the sketches of the new building stucture by hand and with the help of Photoshop, interviewed different students about the topic and co-edited the presentation video using Premiere Pro. Finally, I was in charge of putting together the entire report with Adobe InDesign.
