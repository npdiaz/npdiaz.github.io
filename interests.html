Interests

Sports

Aside from my academic interests, dance has always been one of my biggest passions. I have done ballet since the age of 3 and achieved up to the 8th grade of the Royal Academy of Dance. Horse riding has also been an important sport for me, I have taken part in jumping competitions and attained the "Galope 3" certificate form the Basque Horse Riding Federation.

Since 2014 I started University fitness has become a key part of my life. Being active and healthy is essential for me and helps me succeed in all aspects in my life. I have run two half marathons, five mud races and I have now started to work part-time as a gym instructor.

Volunteering

The most rewarding experiences in my life are linked to volunteering. I discovered this by entering a program in my school that allowed me to do different volunteering activities. I entertained the elderly in a care home for a year and the following one, I was involved in a project that helped immigrants' children to blend in the society.

After my final exams at University I decided to take this a step further by going to Kandi, Benin. There, I joined Vie Por Tous, a local NGO that has schools, boarding schools and medical centers in some cities of the poorest side of the country. I worked in the translation and development of their website, as a manager of the girls dorms and I also taught classes in 3 languages.
